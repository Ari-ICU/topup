# 💰 CLOUDFLARE PRICING CALCULATOR (Top-up Platform)

Since you are using 100% Cloudflare, your monthly cost is likely to be **$0.00** (Zero) until your business grows. Here is the breakdown:

---

### **1. FRONTEND: Cloudflare Pages**
*   **Requests**: Unlimited
*   **Bandwidth**: Unlimited
*   **Builds**: 500 builds per month (Free)
*   **COST**: **$0.00**

---

### **2. BACKEND: Cloudflare Workers**
*   **Requests (Free)**: 100,000 requests per day (About 3 Million per month).
*   **Requests (Paid)**: $5/mo if you need more (Starts at 1 Million / month + $0.30 per million thereafter).
*   **COST**: **$0.00** (Up to 100k visitors/day actions)

---

### **3. DATABASE: Cloudflare D1**
*   **Read Rows (Free)**: 5 Million rows per day.
*   **Write Rows (Free)**: 100,000 rows per day.
*   **Storage (Free)**: 5GB included.
*   **COST**: **$0.00**

---

### **4. STORAGE: Cloudflare R2 (Game Icons & Images)**
*   **Storage (Free)**: First 10 GB per month.
*   **Class A Ops (Write)**: 1 Million per month.
*   **Class B Ops (Read)**: 10 Million per month.
*   **COST**: **$0.00**

---

### **5. DOMAIN: Cloudflare Registrar**
*   **Annual Cost**: Cloudflare charges "Wholesale" prices (no markup).
*   **.com**: ~$9.50 - $11.00 USD per year.
*   **.net / .org**: ~$12.00 - $15.00 USD per year.
*   **COST**: **~ $1.00 / Month** (Paid yearly)

---
